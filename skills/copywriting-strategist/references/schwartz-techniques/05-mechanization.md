# Technique 5: Mechanization

**Source:** *Breakthrough Advertising* by Eugene M. Schwartz, Chapter 11

## Core Principle

Mechanization is the process of verbally proving that your product does what you claim. It's about giving your product's unique mechanism a name and a story, making it the hero of your copy.

## The Three Stages of Mechanization

1.  **Stage One: Name the Mechanism:** Give the unique feature or process that delivers the main benefit a proprietary, memorable name. This turns a commodity into a unique invention.
    *   Example: Instead of "our special blend of herbs," it's "the Synchro-Energize Complex."

2.  **Stage Two: Describe the Mechanism:** Explain how the mechanism works in a simple, clear, and believable way. Use analogies and metaphors to make it easy to understand.

3.  **Stage Three: Feature the Mechanism:** Make the mechanism the star of the show. Show it in action, crushing the prospect's problem and delivering the promised benefits.

## The Importance of the Mechanism

-   **It Creates Uniqueness:** A named mechanism makes your product seem different from all others, even if the underlying technology is similar.
-   **It Builds Belief:** Explaining *how* the product works provides a "reason-why" that makes your claims more believable.
-   **It Justifies the Price:** A unique, proprietary mechanism can justify a higher price point.
