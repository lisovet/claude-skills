# Technique 6: Concentration

**Source:** *Breakthrough Advertising* by Eugene M. Schwartz, Chapter 12

## Core Principle

Concentration is the process of destroying alternate ways for your prospect to satisfy their desire. It's about making your product the one and only solution to their problem.

## How to Destroy Alternatives

1.  **Identify the Alternatives:** What other products or solutions are your prospects considering?

2.  **Acknowledge and Validate:** Show the prospect that you understand their options and that you've considered them too.

3.  **Systematically Disqualify Each Alternative:** For each alternative, explain why it's a poor choice. Use logic, evidence, and testimonials to show its weaknesses.

4.  **Position Your Product as the Superior Solution:** After disqualifying all other options, present your product as the only one that truly solves the problem.

## The Power of Concentration

By systematically eliminating all other options, you create a powerful sense of urgency and inevitability. The prospect is left with only one logical choice: your product.
