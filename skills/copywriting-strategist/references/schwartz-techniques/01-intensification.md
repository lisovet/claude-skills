# Technique 1: Intensification

**Source:** *Breakthrough Advertising* by Eugene M. Schwartz, Chapter 7

## Core Principle

Intensification is the process of strengthening the desire for your product by making its benefits more vivid, more immediate, and more emotionally resonant. It's about taking a simple claim and stretching it in time, space, and imagination.

## The 13 Ways to Strengthen Desire

1.  **Present Your Claims in Action:** Show the product performing, don't just talk about it.
2.  **Bring the Reader into the Action:** Use "you" and "your" to make the reader the hero of the story.
3.  **Show Him How to Test Your Claims:** Give the reader a way to prove your claims for themselves.
4.  **Stretch Out Your Benefits in Time:** Paint a picture of the long-term benefits.
5.  **Bring in an Audience:** Show how the product will earn the reader the admiration of others.
6.  **Show Experts Approving:** Borrow credibility from authorities.
7.  **Compare, Contrast, Prove Superiority:** Show how your product is better than the alternatives.
8.  **Picture the Black Side, Too:** Agitate the pain of not using the product.
9.  **Show How Easy It Is to Get These Benefits:** Remove friction and make the process seem effortless.
10. **Use Metaphor, Analogy, Imagination:** Create powerful mental images.
11. **Before You're Done, Summarize:** Recap the key benefits.
12. **Put Your Guarantee to Work:** Reverse the risk.
13. **How to Apply These Principles of Intensification to the Campaign:** Integrate them into the overall campaign strategy.
