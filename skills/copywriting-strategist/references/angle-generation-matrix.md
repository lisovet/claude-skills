# The Angle Generation Matrix

This document provides the framework and examples for the Angle Generation Matrix, a tool for systematically generating copywriting angles.

## The 3D Matrix

- **Y-Axis (The Customer's Inner World):**
  - Fears & Frustrations
  - Hopes & Desires
  - Beliefs & Identities
  - Social Status & Belonging

- **X-Axis (The Product's Outer World):**
  - Unique Mechanism
  - Key Features
  - Damaging Admissions
  - The Offer

- **Z-Axis (Market Sophistication):**
  - **Level 1 (Pioneering):** Be simple, direct. "What the product does."
  - **Level 2 (Competitive):** Enlarge your claims. "What the product does, better."
  - **Level 3 (Mature):** Introduce a new mechanism. "How the product works."
  - **Level 4 (Hyper-Mature):** Elaborate on the mechanism. "How the product works, better."
  - **Level 5 (Saturated):** Focus on identification. "Who the product is for."

## Example (VitalFuel - Sports Parent)

| | Unique Mechanism | Key Features | Damaging Admissions | The Offer |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Fears** | Fear of unknown chemicals | Fear of sugar crashes | Fear it's not enough carbs | Fear of wasting money |
| **Desires** | Desire for something that works | Desire for simplicity | Desire for honesty | Desire for a good deal |
| **Identity** | "I'm a health-conscious mom" | "I'm a smart shopper" | "I'm a realist" | "I'm a savvy parent" |
| **Status** | Want to be seen as the prepared mom | Want other moms to ask for advice | Want to be seen as authentic | Want to share a find with the team |
