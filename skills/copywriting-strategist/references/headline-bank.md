# Headline Bank

This document contains 100+ proven headline formulas from John Caples and other copywriting masters, categorized by type.

## The Four U's of Headline Writing

- **Urgent:** Creates a sense of immediacy.
- **Unique:** Stands out from the competition.
- **Useful:** Promises a clear benefit.
- **Ultra-specific:** Uses numbers and details to build credibility.

## Headline Formulas

### Question Headlines

1.  Do You Make These Mistakes in [Activity]?
2.  Are You Ashamed of [Problem]?
3.  What Would You Do If [Scenario]?
4.  Who Else Wants [Desirable Outcome]?
5.  How Do You [Achieve Goal]?

### Benefit Headlines

6.  The Secret to [Desirable Outcome].
7.  Get Rid of [Problem] Once and for All.
8.  The Quickest, Easiest Way to [Achieve Goal].
9.  Now You Can [Achieve Goal] Without [Pain Point].
10. [Number] Ways to [Achieve Goal].

### News Headlines

11. Announcing [New Product or Feature].
12. At Last, a [Solution] That [Benefit].
13. The [Industry] Is Talking About [New Trend].
14. Just Released: [New Product].
15. The [Old Way] Is Dead. Here's What's Next.

### How-To Headlines

16. How to [Achieve Goal].
17. How to [Achieve Goal] in [Timeframe].
18. How to [Achieve Goal] Without [Pain Point].
19. How I [Achieved Goal] and How You Can Too.
20. How to [Achieve Goal] Like a [Expert].

### Testimonial Headlines

21. "This [Product] Changed My Life."
22. "I Went from [Problem] to [Solution] in [Timeframe]."
23. "I Never Thought I Could [Achieve Goal] Until I Tried [Product]."
24. "[Authority Figure] Recommends [Product]."
25. "The Only [Product Category] I Trust."
