# Emotional Triggers Library

This document provides a catalog of core human drivers and emotional triggers that can be used to populate the Angle Generation Matrix.

## Core Human Drivers (Maslow, adapted for copywriting)

- **Survival:** Health, safety, security, fear of death or illness.
- **Belonging:** Love, friendship, community, fear of loneliness or rejection.
- **Esteem:** Achievement, status, recognition, respect, fear of failure or humiliation.
- **Self-Actualization:** Personal growth, creativity, fulfilling one's potential.

## The 8 Primal Desires (from "Triggers" by Joe Sugarman)

1.  **Survival, enjoyment of life, life extension.**
2.  **Enjoyment of food and beverages.**
3.  **Freedom from fear, pain, and danger.**
4.  **Sexual companionship.**
5.  **Comfortable living conditions.**
6.  **To be superior, winning, keeping up with the Joneses.**
7.  **Care and protection of loved ones.**
8.  **Social approval.**

## The 9 Learned Desires (Secondary Wants)

1.  **To be informed.**
2.  **Curiosity.**
3.  **Cleanliness of body and surroundings.**
4.  **Efficiency.**
5.  **Convenience.**
6.  **Dependability/quality.**
7.  **Expression of beauty and style.**
8.  **Economy/profit.**
9.  **Bargains.**
